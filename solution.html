Solutions to preserve the Mangroves

  • establishing buffer zones between coastal habitats and adjacent development.
  • identifying and protecting areas where coastal habitats can retreat with sea level rise.
  • Reduce pollution in mangrove environments
  • Don't cut branches or uproot trees for development
  • plant more trees
  • Recycle paper and plastics
  • stop cutting mangroves
